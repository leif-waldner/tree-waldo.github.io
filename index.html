FAQ

  • What is the Local Hack Day?

    The same team that brought you HackCU is hosting a Local Hack Day. It is a beginner friendly event for college students to learn and network with other hackers, makers, and builders. Team sizes are up to 4 people.

  • When is HackCU?

    HackCU's Local Hack day kicks off the morning of of Saturday, October 10th and ends the same evening. A full schedule will be published at a later date.

  • What Should I Bring?

    Everything you need for 12 hours of hacking. Bring all your electronics: Chargers, laptop, headphones, powerstrip, whatever you need. Food and drink will be provided all day.

  • Am I Allowed to Use Past Projects?

    Feel free. Although the official HackCU hackathon in the spring will be a competition, you are free to do whatever you'd like at the local hack day!

  • What is the Cost of Attending?

    It's completely free. We'll provide food and all other associated costs for the entire weekend.

  • I'm in. Now what?

    Create and register for HackCU on ChallengePost. Most everything will be here from now on.
